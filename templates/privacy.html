Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

We collect the information you provide when you create an account, including your name and email address. We also store the expense records you log — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions — as well as basic usage data such as login timestamps.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. This includes displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and maintaining your account. We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond the core functionality of the app.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal or financial data with third parties. We do not integrate with advertising networks, analytics platforms, or data brokers. If this changes in the future, we will update this policy and notify you in advance.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at privacy@spendly.app. We aim to respond to all inquiries within 48 hours.
